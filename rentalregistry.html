Rental Registry

This page was made for the Ministry of Social Services with the intention of helping citizens find affordable locations to live and work in the Azalea Isles and provides useful information on plots/regions meeting the following requirements:

  • it is a rentable property
  • there is no current tenant
  • rent duration is at least one day
  • rent price is $1 or higher

This data is automatically gathered from CityRP's APIs and may be temporarily unavailable if either the API or our own backend experiences technical difficulties. Ordinarily, in-game changes should be reflected in this registry within one hour. In case of lasting unavailability or other issues, please reach out to the CityRP Ministry of Social Services.
